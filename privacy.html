Sudoku Shisho Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 3, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku Shisho collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application available on iOS. By using Sudoku Shisho, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We minimize data collection to provide a seamless gaming experience. Below are the types of information we may collect:

  • Anonymous Gameplay Data:
    • No personal information is required to play Sudoku Shisho.
    • A device-based identifier is encrypted using a secure XOR cipher and stored in our Appwrite backend to manage game progress.
    • For legacy data, unencrypted identifiers may exist but are transitioned to encrypted storage.
  • Sign in with Apple (Optional):
    • If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, we receive a unique, anonymized user ID to sync your game progress across devices.
    • You can opt to share your email or use Apple’s private email relay to hide your email address.
  • Multiplayer Data:
    • For multiplayer modes (Time Attack, Battle, Co-op), we collect temporary session data (e.g., encrypted player IDs, scores, and game state) to facilitate gameplay over Bluetooth or internet connections.
    • No personally identifiable information is stored for multiplayer sessions.
  • In-App Purchases:
    • Purchase data (e.g., for "Beginner Sudoku Boards") is processed by Apple and managed under their Privacy Policy.
    • We do not collect payment details or financial information.
  • Support Requests:
    • If you contact us via email (support@sudokushisho.com), we may collect your email address and information you provide to resolve your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Sudoku Shisho gaming experience.
  • Sync progress across devices when using Sign in with Apple.
  • Facilitate multiplayer modes by managing temporary session data.
  • Respond to support inquiries and troubleshoot issues.

We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third parties, except as described below.

3. Data Sharing

  • Apple: Sign in with Apple and in-app purchase data are managed by Apple under their Privacy Policy.
  • Service Providers: We use Appwrite for secure data storage, with device identifiers encrypted using a Web Crypto API-based XOR cipher.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Device-based identifiers are encrypted using a Web Crypto API-based XOR cipher with a 256-bit key stored securely in your device’s iOS Keychain.
  • Legacy unencrypted data is supported for backward compatibility but transitioned to encrypted storage upon access.
  • Multiplayer session data is encrypted during transmission over secure HTTPS connections.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

  • Sign in with Apple: You can play anonymously without signing in, or use Apple’s private email relay to hide your email.
  • Data Deletion: To delete your game progress, uninstall the app or clear app data via Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Sudoku Shisho. Contact us to delete server-stored data.
  • Support Data: Contact us to request deletion of any data provided through support inquiries.

6. Children’s Privacy

Sudoku Shisho is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@sudokushisho.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
