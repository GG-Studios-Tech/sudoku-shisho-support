Sudoku Shisho Support

Welcome to the official support page for Sudoku Shisho, your ultimate Sudoku experience! This page provides guidance on using the app, troubleshooting common issues, and contacting our support team.

About Sudoku Shisho

Sudoku Shisho is a mobile Sudoku game designed for iPhone and iPad, offering a rich and engaging puzzle experience. Key features include:

  • Single-Player Mode: Solve classic 9x9 Sudoku puzzles at various difficulty levels, from beginner to expert.
  • Multiplayer Modes:
    • Time Attack: Coming Soon.
  • In-App Purchases: Unlock additional content, such as "Beginner Sudoku Boards" (10 boards for $0.99), to enhance your gameplay.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for both iPhone and iPad with adaptive layouts for seamless gameplay.

Getting Started

  1. Installation:
    • Download Sudoku Shisho from the App Store or TestFlight (for beta testers).
    • Ensure your device runs iOS 15.0 or later for optimal performance.
  2. Account Setup:
    • Use Sign in with Apple for optional progress syncing across devices.
    • Alternatively, play anonymously with a device-based identifier—no email required.
  3. Gameplay:
    • Select a mode from the main menu (Single-Player).

Troubleshooting

  • App Crashes or Freezes:
    • Ensure your device is updated to the latest iOS version.
    • Restart the app or device.
    • Clear the app cache: Go to Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Sudoku Shisho > Offload App, then reinstall.
  • In-App Purchase Issues:
    • Confirm your payment method is valid in the App Store.
    • Check for purchase restoration: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases.
    • Contact Apple Support for billing issues.
  • Sign in with Apple:
    • If syncing fails, log out and log back in via Settings > Sign in with Apple.
    • For anonymous play, no sign-in is required.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Is an account required to play?
    No, you can play anonymously. Sign in with Apple is optional for syncing progress.
  • How do I access multiplayer modes?
    Navigate to the multiplayer section in the app, select a mode, and connect via Bluetooth or internet. Invite friends or join public matches.
  • What are the system requirements?
    Sudoku Shisho requires iOS 15.0 or later and supports iPhone and iPad. For multiplayer, a stable internet or Bluetooth connection is needed.
  • How do power-ups work in Battle Mode?
    Power-ups (e.g., hints or time freezes) can be earned or purchased to gain an edge. They’re activated during your turn to affect scoring or gameplay.
